about us

Google Developer Groups on Campus are community groups for college and university students interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome.

benefits of GDG Club

  • /01

    Gain hands-on experience with the latest Google technologies and tools.

  • /02

    Network with like-minded developers and industry professionals.

  • /03

    Participate in hackathons, workshops, and collaborative coding sessions.

  • /04

    Access exclusive resources and events through GDG chapters globally.

  • /05

    Build and showcase projects to enhance your portfolio and skills.

  • /06

    Receive mentorship and guidance from experienced developers and experts.
